Movments
Movment is esential to this game, you need to understand something here:
First of all you need to remember all of car racing game, and more exactly, Corner Cuttig
It will save you some time then you go to your position, 1 second or 10th of second, whatever
With this free time you can easily outrun enemy, and take them by surprise or buy some time for yourself if you are on CT side
Beside that, if you don't have intel on enemies, try to bite on corners fastly moving beside it
If some enemy will check your position, no matter how high skill he or she is, a good bite can't be prefired or readed
And more importantly, enemy with high chances will shoot one your position, revealing himself by tracer or sound
So now you have intel on enemy location, well done
Another form of movment is strafe, where a side velocity is combined with forewerd one what give you some bust in speed
most helpfull this one is for Corner Cuttig and Bunny Hopping
Exemple of Strafe
Bunny Hopping is action there you rapidly jump, summing up speed from air acceleration and strafing
You can rapidly jump by binding mouse wheel for jump
Notice, that if you will fail you grip on jump you will lose acceleration and will be slowed for brief moment
BH is considerd very difficult movment technic, so don't worry very much about constantly loosing grip on jump
Some tips for MM:
----
-> Don't use BH if you can't make 4 consequtive jumps frequently
----
-> MM has a velocity limiter what deny air strafe acceleration
Exemple of BH, Bunny Hope
Beside everything that have been said above:
----
-> Try not to jump if you don't want to Cut a Corner or Bunny Hoop
----
-> MM has a velocity limiter what deny air strafe acceleration
----
-> When baiting, don't try to repeat this action in same rythm because enemy will read your next movment and prefire
----
and most classical one

Utility
Utility is referred to granades in this game and they are far more usefull then an beginer player can imagine and
First of all, always buy granade, at least 1 smoke for CT or 1 flesh for T
Secondly, you can throw granade in three modes: Slow (Right Click), with Medium Strenght (Right Click + Left Click) and Strong throw (Left Click)
Smoke are most easiest utilitis to use, they are used mostly for bloking view on two or more sided position
On higher levels, smokes are used to stop T from pushing, becouse of mechanics of smoke in this game
Person that exit from smoke will see another from outside with delay but from outside, enemies will see without delay
Exemple of "Smoking position"
so teammates can push with no worries about crossfire from enemies
Flesh are most esentil utilitis in game, they are harder to master becouse they need to be thrown specialy so enemies will not see them fast enoght to react (to turn around from them)
Most basic flesh throw is to throw it stright up, this will flesh everyone who is not looking down
More advenced flesh is Corner flesh what is thrown with low strenght so it will fly slow enought to not be to loud, and to suprise enemies with "POP-flesh"
A Exemple of Slow throw
Molotov/Incendiery have 2 propouse, to make enemies leave their camping positions (T), and to make enemies slow down their push (CT)
A Terrorist throw a molotov on standard camping position
HE (High Explosive) or how people name them 'nade, are used to damage enemys that are hiding behind the wall or obstacle
Nade's can be used to delay T-push, but I recommand to save nade's to later minutes of round
There they can save your life if you know there enemies are
Last granade in list is decoy granade, rarly used and prety much bought randomly becouse of small price of 50$
Can be usefull to use against Pro player what try to read situation on the map and momentary turn around from flash granade
In this situation you can make an agressive push with decoy but it's very dangerous to do so becouse not everyone in this game are good player, yeah...
A good Exemple of CT execute

Play-Styles
What is a play-style of the?
Well, first of all it's play-style of whole team, so
If there is someone who try to rebel in your team agains your team play style
you will not win a single game
Yes, this game is team-based, you need fullparty of 5 friends to win.
An interest thing in this game are Fake's
This consist mainly from utility thrown on another site to disorient and missinform enemies
Which can lead to suprise push and advantage for (mostly) T side
So let's continue, there are common (economical one's) play-styles and some uniqu to CT and T sides:
Common:
♻️ --- Eco --- ♻️
Round there you don't buy anything to save money for later game
But i recommand to buy p250 becouse it's cheap and quite powerfull for 300$
This kind of rounds are not meant to be won, and there is some common missunderstanding among many player
You need to play more passivly, becouse if you will rush on enemies without anything beside pistol, enemies will gain fast-income killing you
Be a rat, be a camper and don't be ashamed for doing so
✊ --- Force --- ✊
Round there your team don't have money for Rifle+Cevlar combo, so everyone are buying farmguns+kevlar
If your team is professiongal enoght, you can ignore "eco-round to fullbuy" setup
But be carefull, if you lose a force, your team will not have enought money even on SMG next round
🌀 --- Full-Buy --- 🌀
Classical round, everyone have rifle+kevlar combo and additionaly, full granade stack and pistol
½ --- Semi-Buy --- ½
Typicle happen on non-full party
Someone in team have a full-buy but other teammates have money only for force, I don't recommand this kind of tactics

CT:
=== Counter-Push ===
Round there CT try to capture positions of T, using utility and rush, doing so will probably take enemies by surprise, but, don't forget
If you are doing a stack counter-push (5 person push) you have 50/50 to not guess where terrorist are pushing (if they are doing so) B or A ?
and if doing solo push or with one-two teammates, you can end be overruned by stack of enemies
=== Stack ===
Round there CT guard only one site in 5, this is an risky tactic and is mainly used on eco rounds or more rarly seen on force rounds
What to say about it ? 50/50 chance to guess and if enemies have good intel, this chances go stright down to 0%

T:
/// Rush \\\
Simple as hello world, stack of 5 terrorists rush A or B site with minimal utility execute,
mainly executed wrong becouse of poor commuication between teammates
this play-style is most difficult to master, everyone need to be prepared to fast reaction, fast adaptation and improvisation on place, if someone in team will forgot or will do actions with minimal delay, everything will go wrong, I bet you it will
/// Execute \\\
Sometimes stack, sometimes no, an push with smokes and molotovs to drawback enemies from camping positions and to free site to plant a bomb
Most classical play-style of terrorists, often paired with fake utility and fack pushes
/// Freeplay \\\
Round there T spred across the map and are trying to find open-frags to open a sites
Mainly used on non-team party and casual servers of CSGO, not recommanded for MM
/// Lowbeats \\\
Round there T hold position to last minute, similar to Freeplay with difference that Lowbeats are played more passivly
This kind of tactics is used on eco rounds or if CT have been pushing and counter-rushing very often
